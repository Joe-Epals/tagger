Tagger Overview

The Tagger Window is composed of three panes.

The Tabs pane determines the mode in which you are working:

  • Click the Tag tab to select the resource(s) for which you are entering information or which you are saving. You must select the check-box next to the relevant resources to let Tagger know what is active during this tagging session.
  • Click the Output tab to confirm or review your work prior to saving your work out to an external file.

The Detail pane displays the fields in which the tag data will be entered. The fields are grouped into three tabs on this pane, the General tab, Education tab, and the Alignment tab.

The Resource pane displays the web page for the educational resource as specified by the URL entered on the General tab on the Detail pane.
